REDESIGN OF CASTRILLODE RIOPISUERGA

A rebranding project for Castrillo de Pisuerga focused on simplifying its coat of arms into a more contemporary visual language and creating an identity system designed to increase the town’s visibility. The project explored graphic synthesis, symbolic reduction, and flexible applications to build a clearer, more recognisable, and more current representation of the village.
