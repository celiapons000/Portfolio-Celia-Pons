REBRANDING CHIMPATÍA

A branding project developed for Chimpatía, focused on building a warm, approachable, and emotionally driven identity. The project explores expressive illustration, playful compositions, and a visual system that reinforces empathy, connection, and social awareness.

CATEGORY

Brand Identity

YEAR

2025

CLIENT

Academic Project

THE CHALLENGE

Create a visual identity that communicates empathy and emotional connection while remaining clear, flexible, and recognizable across different formats and audiences.

THE SOLUTION

We developed an expressive identity system based on illustration, organic shapes, and warm color palettes. The system balances playfulness and clarity, allowing it to adapt across different communication contexts.
