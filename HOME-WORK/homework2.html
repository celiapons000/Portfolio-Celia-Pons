REBRANDING TEATROS DEL CANAL

A visual identity project for Teatros del Canal focused on building a clearer, more contemporary, and more flexible communication system. The project explored a typographic language, modular compositions, campaign applications, and a bold institutional presence adaptable across digital and physical formats.

CATEGORY

Brand Identity

YEAR

2025

CLIENT

Escuela Superior de Diseño Madrid

THE CHALLENGE

Create a contemporary and memorable visual identity for Teatros del Canal that improves clarity and recognition while respecting its cultural and institutional character. The system needed to work across campaigns, signage, editorial formats, and digital touchpoints.

THE SOLUTION

We developed a typographic and modular visual system designed to feel bold, flexible, and culturally relevant. The proposal combines strong structural compositions, a reduced but distinctive color palette, and scalable layouts that allow the identity to adapt across different seasons, formats, and communication needs.
