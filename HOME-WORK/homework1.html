REBRANDING BANDCAMP

A comprehensive brand identity project for Bandcamp, redefining it as a professional niche-music platform rooted in community and music culture. The project included a flexible modular identity system (patterns and characters), typography, color palette, motion direction, and brand guidelines.

CATEGORY

Brand Identity

YEAR

2026

CLIENT

Escuela Superior de Diseño Madrid

THE CHALLENGE

Create a flexible, memorable brand identity for Bandcamp that brings clarity and professionalism to the platform while preserving its handmade, niche culture and community-driven spirit, bridging discovery with real support for artists, labels, and fans.

THE SOLUTION

We developed a flexible identity system built from modular, handmade graphic elements that capture Bandcamp’s niche music culture while improving clarity and professionalism. A clean neo-grotesk type base and warm earth-tone palette are paired with motion and music-reactive icons to keep the system consistent and adaptable across digital and physical applications.
